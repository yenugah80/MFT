## Headline
**Questions? We've got answers.**

### How is this different from MyFitnessPal?
MyFitnessPal shows you calories and macros from a database. MyFoodTracker learns YOUR body's specific responses—which foods improve YOUR mood, YOUR energy, YOUR focus. After 30 days, you have insights no generic app can provide.

### How accurate is the AI?
Every insight shows its confidence level. The AI starts with research-backed estimates and improves with YOUR data. Typical users see 80-90% confidence after 30 days of logging.

### What data do you collect?
Only what you log: meals, water, mood, activity. We never sell your data. You can export or delete everything anytime. Full GDPR compliance.

### Does it work with my diet?
Yes. MyFoodTracker understands 200+ cuisines, dietary restrictions, and eating styles. Whether you're keto, vegan, intermittent fasting, or just eating balanced—it adapts to YOU.

### How long until I see insights?
Basic patterns emerge in 1-2 weeks. Personalized correlations with high confidence typically appear after 3-4 weeks of regular logging.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. Bank-level encryption, GDPR compliance, and you control your data. We never share with third parties.
