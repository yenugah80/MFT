# PITCH DECK TIPS

## Delivery Guidance

**Timing:**
- Aim for 3-4 minutes per slide
- Total presentation: 10-15 minutes
- Leave 15+ minutes for Q&A

**Common Investor Questions to Prepare:**
1. Why will users pay for this vs free alternatives?
2. How do you acquire users cost-effectively?
3. What's your unfair advantage / moat?
4. Why are you the right person to build this?
5. What happens if MyFitnessPal copies this?
6. How do you handle data privacy / GDPR?
7. What are your key risks?

**Key Numbers to Know Cold:**
- TAM/SAM/SOM
- CAC and LTV
- Current MRR/ARR
- Retention rates
- Burn rate / runway
- Competition pricing

# DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS

## Visual Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #10B981 (Emerald green - health, growth)
- Secondary: #8B5CF6 (Purple - intelligence, premium)
- Accent: #F59E0B (Amber - energy, highlights)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, 32-48pt
- Body: Regular, 18-24pt
- Keep text minimal - let visuals tell the story

**Each Slide Should Have:**
- One main message
- Supporting visual (chart, screenshot, icon)
- Minimal text (6-8 bullet points max)

**Tools to Build:**
- Figma (free, collaborative)
- Pitch (pitch.com - modern templates)
- Canva (easy, templates)
